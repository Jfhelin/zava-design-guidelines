# Zava Style

This file defines the workshop Zava design language.

This is a workshop-defined brand direction inferred from the provided Zava demo references and logo samples. It should be treated as the source of truth for workshop branding unless replaced by more specific guidance.

## Design Intent

- trustworthy
- calm
- modern
- approachable
- clean and product-focused
- premium but not flashy
- monochrome and crisp rather than colorful

## Visual Traits

- dark teal header and footer blocks
- light gray-blue page background with white surfaces
- strong spacing rhythm
- clear section hierarchy
- black or charcoal text and structural lines
- clear cards and panels
- clean, readable system typography
- rounded corners and softened edges
- compact but breathable layouts
- Bootstrap-like control styling with light borders

## Brand Shape

- the primary logo is a black circular `Z` mark attached to a rounded rectangular `ava` wordmark
- the logo should appear on white or very light neutral surfaces
- the logo should feel crisp, simple, horizontal, and high-contrast
- default brand expression is monochrome rather than accent-color driven

## Product Feel

- resembles a polished self-service app more than a marketing page
- hero areas can exist, but they should support tasks, not dominate the screen
- account screens, sign-in surfaces, and product grids should feel orderly and lightweight
- headers often combine a dark brand band with a simple navigation bar
- footers should be substantial and structured, not minimal one-line footers

## Fonts

- use the system UI stack
- base body size is 14px
- headings are modest in size, not oversized display typography
- prefer medium or semibold weights over heavy marketing typography

## Color Anchors

- dark teal: `#1C3D4B`
- page background: `#EBEFF3`
- footer brand heading: `#BDD5E0`
- footer section accent: `#9BCBE1`
- muted body copy: `#696E6F`
- footer links: `#A6A49D`
- brand primary accent: `#427187`

## Avoid

- noisy gradients
- glassmorphism
- playful cartoon illustration
- exaggerated animation
- dark-heavy dashboards unless specifically requested
- crowded interfaces
- recoloring the logo into arbitrary accent colors
